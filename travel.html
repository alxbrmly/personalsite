About

I recently visited San Diego and other areas in the southern California region and loved it! The weather was fair, the people were friendly, and there were palm trees and cacti everywhere. I went to several beaches, so many I can't remember all their names, but who cares? I had fun! I did the tourist thing and visited Gaslamp in San Diego, Cabrillo monument, and Griffith Observatory right outside LA. The views were breath-taking.

While in California, I was able to enjoy tons of delicious Mexican food! I ate veggie burritos almost everyday, actually every day. The burritos were huge and half the price of a burrito in NYC, and of course way more delicious. I also found vegan donuts in downtown San Deigo, and they were not only tasty, but large and still cheaper than those here in New York.

I didn't buy much while in California, I mostly window shopped because I spent most of my time outdoors. I did manage to check out the Outlets at the Border and they were okay. They're not as amazing as Woodbury Commons in New York. I think the outlet are for actual residents because the clothing definitely would not suit colder weather. The coolest part about these particular outlets is that they literally are across from the Mexican border.
